Project - Wingspan User Importer
Technology Used - Python, Node.js, PostgreSQL
Infosys Wingspan is a versatile learning solution that helps organizations accelerate their talent transformation and change journey. Wingspan needs to manage users access on the platform by regularly syncing with client data.

-It is responsible for removing access of users for whom the client wants to remove access.
The project was to design, program, and make it extensible to adapt to future enhancements.
